MWE and sentence rater

User interface for CEFR ratings of MWEs and sentences.

Please select your identifier and make a choice between multi-word expressions (MWEs) and sentences. You will be shown one item at a time and asked to rate it for understandeability by learners of different proficiency levels.

Please note that it is not possible to go back to previous items.

If you want to save your progress, click on the "Save/Stop" button on the right. If you use the same device to visit the page again, you can resume from where you stopped.
